The Earthling

Solo Game Project – Unity | Art | Code | Animation

The Earthling is a solo-developed game made entirely in Unity, featuring original art, animations, and gameplay mechanics built from the ground up. This trailer showcases the atmosphere, mechanics, and unique tone of the game.
