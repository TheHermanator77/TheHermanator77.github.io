Interactive Quantum Mechanics Comic

Team Project – Story • Animation • UI • Science Concepts

This superhero-themed comic introduces readers to quantum mechanics concepts like entanglement and phasing. I contributed animation, programming, UI, background design, and lore-based humor throughout the comic experience.

My Contributions

Quantum Minigame (Panel 11): I programmed this game to visualize quantum entanglement. The player controls both characters at once using mirrored movement to escape the burning building — representing how entangled particles move in opposite ways.

Panels 8 & 9: I designed the background and visual transition between these two scenes. The change in flooring and lighting signifies a room shift as Probabella awakens to her quantum powers and phases through the floor.

Gluon News (News Animation): I created the news channel logo “Gluon News” — inspired by gluons transmitting quantum information — and contributed humor and lore to the text scroll for worldbuilding and tone.
