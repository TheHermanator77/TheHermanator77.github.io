Interactive Quantum Mechanics Comic

Team Project – Story • Animation • UI • Science Concepts

This superhero-themed comic explains various quantum mechanics concepts through a narrative origin story. The story follows a young hero named Probabella as she awakens to her powers and gradually learns how to manipulate quantum principles like entanglement and phasing. The comic combines gameplay, storytelling, and educational content to make complex ideas accessible and engaging.

My Contributions

Quantum Minigame (Panel 11): I designed and programmed this minigame as a platforming puzzle to demonstrate quantum entanglement. The player guides a citizen through a burning building using maze-like movement, while watching how objects entangled with each other move in mirrored or opposing directions. This allowed players to understand entanglement through interactive gameplay.

Panels 8 & 9: I created the background and scene transitions for this sequence, where Probabella first awakens to her quantum powers. These panels depict a dramatic shift in tone and lighting, showing the moment her abilities activate and set the stage for the powers she’ll develop throughout the story.

Gluon News (News Animation): I created this in-universe news segment to provide world-building and humor. I designed the animation, developed the scroll text, and invented the name "Gluon News" — a reference to gluons, which exchange quantum information between particles. This segment helps flesh out the game's setting while teaching scientific concepts in a fun way.
